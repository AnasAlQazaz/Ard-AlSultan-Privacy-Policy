Last Updated: March 2026

English

1. Data Collection

The Ard Al Sultan application collects minimal information required for vehicle logistics tracking. We only store your provided Phone Number and Vehicle Identification Numbers (VINs) assigned to you by the administration.

2. Use of Data

Your data is strictly used to provide tracking updates, shipping status, and push notifications regarding your specific vehicles. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties.

3. Account & Data Deletion

You have the right to request the complete deletion of your account and data from our systems at any time. You can initiate this process directly within the app via the "Request Account Deletion" button in the menu, or by contacting the developer directly at anasharith2@gmail.com.
